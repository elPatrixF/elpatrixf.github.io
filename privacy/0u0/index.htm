Privacy policy

This application is meant to be a cute face. It does not do anything else. Uses the internal gyroscope/accelerometer to detect orientation.

Data collected by the app

I have not programmed this app to collect any personally identifiable information. Settings are not saved when the app closes and it can be safely used without internet connection.

This app also does not offer any kind of monetization feature, nor accepts any payments in any form. This app shall be distributed for free, with no analytics of any kind implemented on it.
